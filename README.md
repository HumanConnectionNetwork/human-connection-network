# Human Connection Network

> **A Human Infrastructure where Trust is the Protocol.**

Human Connection Network (HCN) is an open, nonprofit initiative that connects people who want to help directly with people who need support.

Our mission is simple:

**Reduce the distance between people through technology while preserving dignity, transparency, privacy and trust.**

---

# Vision

We believe technology should make human solidarity easier, not more complicated.

Instead of becoming another intermediary, Human Connection Network exists to enable direct human-to-human connections.

We do not seek to replace humanitarian organizations.

We do not seek to replace governments.

We do not seek to centralize donations.

We seek to build the digital infrastructure that allows people to help one another directly.

---

# Core Principles

## Human First

Technology exists to serve people.

Every design decision must prioritize human dignity over efficiency.

---

## Trust by Design

Trust is not requested.

Trust is built.

Verification, transparency and accountability are part of the system itself.

---

## Transparency

Operational resources belong to the community.

Platform expenses will always be publicly available.

Transparency is a feature, not a report.

---

## Privacy

Helping someone should never require exposing their dignity.

Only the minimum necessary information should be public.

---

## Direct Human Connection

Whenever possible, people should be able to connect directly.

The platform exists to facilitate connections.

Not to replace them.

---

## Open Collaboration

Knowledge should be shared.

Documentation should be public.

Standards should be open.

Innovation should benefit everyone.

---

# What Human Connection Network is NOT

Human Connection Network is **not**:

- A crowdfunding platform.
- A payment processor.
- A bank.
- A fundraising company.
- A social network.

Human Connection Network is an infrastructure for human connection.

---

# Current Projects

## Red Conexión Humana

The first implementation of Human Connection Network.

Focused on enabling direct assistance between people affected by humanitarian emergencies and those willing to help.

---

## Humanitarian Connection Protocol (HCP)

An open protocol that will allow humanitarian platforms to exchange verified information using a common standard.

The goal is interoperability.

Not centralization.

---

## Open Humanitarian Network

A future ecosystem of organizations, developers and communities collaborating through open standards.

---

# Long-Term Vision

We envision a world where humanitarian technology is:

- Open
- Transparent
- Interoperable
- Community-driven
- Accessible
- Privacy-respecting

Our ambition is not to build the biggest platform.

Our ambition is to build infrastructure that anyone can use.

---

# Roadmap

- Build the first public platform (Red Conexión Humana)
- Validate real-world humanitarian connections
- Publish open technical documentation
- Design the Humanitarian Connection Protocol (HCP)
- Open the protocol to community collaboration
- Build an international ecosystem

---

# Open Source Philosophy

Human Connection Network is built with openness in mind.

Whenever possible:

- Documentation will be public.
- Standards will be open.
- Community contributions will be encouraged.
- Decisions will be documented.

---

# Contributing

This project welcomes developers, designers, humanitarian workers, translators, researchers and anyone willing to help.

Technology alone cannot solve humanitarian challenges.

People can.

Technology simply helps them find each other.
